# Purpose

This folder serves as the central database for all products evaluated by Trix Commerce AI OS.

---

# Objective

Maintain accurate records of products, suppliers, competitors, pricing, and evaluation scores to support data-driven business decisions.

---

# Contents

- Master Product Database
- Product Scorecards
- Supplier Database
- Competitor Database

---

# Workflow

1. Discover Product
2. Apply Market Filters
3. Complete Product Evaluation
4. Compare Competitors
5. Verify Supplier
6. Add Approved Product to Database

---

# Goal

Build a searchable product database that grows with the business and supports future product launches.
