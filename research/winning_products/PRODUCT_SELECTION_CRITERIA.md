# Product Selection Criteria

## Version

v1.0

---

# Purpose

Define the mandatory criteria that every product must meet before being added to the Trix Commerce store.

---

# Market Demand

Requirements

- Active customer demand
- Evergreen or consistently popular
- Suitable for the Pakistani market

Status

Mandatory

---

# Profit Margin

Requirements

- Minimum Gross Profit: 40%
- Healthy pricing flexibility
- Room for advertising costs

Status

Mandatory

---

# Competition

Requirements

- Moderate competition preferred
- Avoid highly saturated products
- Opportunity for differentiation

Status

Mandatory

---

# Supplier Quality

Requirements

- Reliable supplier
- Good product quality
- Stable inventory
- Fast processing time

Status

Mandatory

---

# Shipping

Requirements

- Lightweight
- Low shipping cost
- Suitable for Cash on Delivery
- Low damage risk

Status

Mandatory

---

# Return Risk

Requirements

- Low defect rate
- Easy to explain
- Low customer confusion

Status

Mandatory

---

# Customer Value

Requirements

The product should solve a real problem or provide clear value.

Status

Mandatory

---

# Product Rating

Minimum Target Rating

4.5 / 5

---

# Decision Rule

A product must satisfy all mandatory criteria before moving to the next evaluation stage.
