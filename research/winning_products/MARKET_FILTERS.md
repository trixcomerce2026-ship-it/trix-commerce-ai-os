# Market Filters

## Version

v1.0

---

# Purpose

Define which products Trix Commerce should actively pursue and which products should be avoided.

---

# Target Market

Country

Pakistan

Primary Customers

- Men
- Women
- Families
- Students
- Office Workers

Sales Model

Shopify + Cash on Delivery (COD)

---

# Approved Categories

- Home & Kitchen
- Home Organization
- Smart Home Accessories
- Mobile Accessories
- Car Accessories
- Beauty Tools (non-medical)
- Pet Accessories
- Fitness Accessories
- Baby Accessories (safe products)
- Office & Desk Accessories

---

# Avoid Categories

- Medicines
- Supplements
- Food Items
- Fragile Glass Products
- Hazardous Materials
- Counterfeit Products
- Adult Products
- Weapons
- Tobacco & Vape Products
- Copyrighted Merchandise

---

# Product Price Range

Ideal Selling Price

PKR 1,500 – PKR 5,000

---

# Product Size

Preferred

- Small
- Lightweight
- Easy to Pack

Avoid

- Bulky
- Heavy
- Oversized

---

# Product Characteristics

Preferred

- Solves a real problem
- Easy to explain
- Visually appealing
- Demonstrates well in short videos
- Low return rate

Avoid

- Complex setup
- High maintenance
- Seasonal demand only
- Easily damaged in shipping

---

# Supplier Preference

- Reliable communication
- Consistent stock
- Quality packaging
- Fast dispatch

---

# Final Rule

Only products that satisfy these filters should move to detailed evaluation.
