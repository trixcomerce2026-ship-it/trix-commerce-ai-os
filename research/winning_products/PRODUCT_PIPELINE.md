# Product Pipeline

## Version

v1.0

---

# Purpose

Track every product from discovery to launch using a standardized workflow.

---

# Pipeline Stages

## Stage 1 – Product Discovery

Status

- New Product Found

Tasks

- Record product name
- Record source
- Record discovery date

---

## Stage 2 – Market Filters

Tasks

- Check approved category
- Check target price range
- Check shipping suitability
- Check product characteristics

Decision

Pass / Reject

---

## Stage 3 – Product Evaluation

Tasks

- Complete evaluation template
- Calculate profit margin
- Score product
- Review supplier

Decision

Approve / Reject

---

## Stage 4 – Competitor Research

Tasks

- Identify competitors
- Review pricing
- Analyze strengths
- Analyze weaknesses

Decision

Competitive / Needs Improvement

---

## Stage 5 – Product Preparation

Tasks

- Create Shopify listing
- Prepare images
- Write product description
- Generate marketing assets

Status

Ready for Launch

---

## Stage 6 – Store Launch

Tasks

- Publish product
- Configure shipping
- Enable payment methods
- Verify product page

Status

Live

---

## Stage 7 – Performance Monitoring

Monitor

- Website Visitors
- Add-to-Cart Rate
- Conversion Rate
- Orders
- Customer Reviews
- Return Rate

Decision

Scale / Optimize / Remove

---

# Pipeline Rules

- Every product must complete each stage before moving to the next.
- Rejected products should be documented with the reason for rejection.
- Approved products should be added to the product database.
