# Shopify Customer Journey

## Version

v1.0

---

# Purpose

Define the complete customer journey from discovering the store to becoming a repeat customer.

---

# Stage 1 – Discovery

Customers find Trix Commerce through:

- Facebook Ads
- TikTok Videos
- Instagram Posts
- WhatsApp Sharing
- Google Search

Goal:
Bring targeted visitors to the Shopify store.

---

# Stage 2 – Landing

Customer arrives on:

- Homepage
OR
- Product Page

The page should clearly show:

- Product benefits
- High-quality images
- Competitive pricing
- Customer trust badges
- Clear Call-to-Action (CTA)

Goal:
Capture the visitor's interest.

---

# Stage 3 – Exploration

Customer browses:

- Product Collections
- Related Products
- Best Sellers
- New Arrivals

Goal:
Encourage customers to explore more products.

---

# Stage 4 – Purchase Decision

Customer checks:

- Product Description
- Features
- Specifications
- Reviews (Future)
- Shipping Information
- Return Policy

Goal:
Build confidence before purchase.

---

# Stage 5 – Checkout

Customer:

- Adds product to cart
- Enters shipping details
- Selects Cash on Delivery (COD)
- Confirms order

Goal:
Complete the purchase with minimal friction.

---

# Stage 6 – Order Processing

Store:

- Receives the order
- Confirms the order
- Packs the product
- Ships it through the courier partner

Goal:
Deliver quickly and accurately.

---

# Stage 7 – Delivery

Customer receives the order.

Goal:
Provide a positive unboxing experience.

---

# Stage 8 – After-Sales

Actions:

- Ask for customer feedback
- Resolve issues promptly
- Encourage repeat purchases
- Offer related products

Goal:
Increase customer satisfaction and loyalty.

---

# Customer Journey Principles

- Build trust at every step.
- Keep the process simple.
- Communicate clearly.
- Make mobile shopping easy.
- Deliver excellent customer support.
