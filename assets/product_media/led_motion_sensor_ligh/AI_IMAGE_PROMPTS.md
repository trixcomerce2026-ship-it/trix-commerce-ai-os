# Purpose

Professional AI prompts for creating Shopify-ready product images.

---

## Prompt 1 – Main Product Image

Create a high-resolution e-commerce product photo of a wireless LED motion sensor light on a pure white background. Show the product from the front with soft studio lighting, realistic shadows, sharp details, no watermark, no text, and a premium modern appearance. Square composition suitable for a Shopify product page.

---

## Prompt 2 – Lifestyle Image

Create a realistic lifestyle image of a wireless LED motion sensor light installed inside a modern wardrobe in a clean Pakistani-style home. The light should automatically illuminate clothes with warm white lighting. The room should look modern, tidy, and welcoming. No people, no text, photorealistic quality.

---

## Prompt 3 – Kitchen Use

Create a realistic image of a wireless LED motion sensor light installed beneath a kitchen cabinet, illuminating the countertop. Modern kitchen, clean environment, warm lighting, premium product photography style, no text.

---

## Prompt 4 – Staircase Use

Create a realistic home staircase illuminated by multiple wireless LED motion sensor lights. Warm evening atmosphere, safe walking path, modern interior design, photorealistic, no people, no text.

---

## Prompt 5 – Packaging

Create a clean product packaging presentation showing the wireless LED motion sensor light, accessories, and retail box neatly arranged on a white background. High-resolution commercial product photography.

---

# General Style Guidelines

- Photorealistic
- High resolution
- Clean background
- Soft natural lighting
- Modern home environment
- Mobile-friendly composition
- No watermark
- No logos
- No text inside the image
