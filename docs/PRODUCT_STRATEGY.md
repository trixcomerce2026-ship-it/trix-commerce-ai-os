# Product Strategy

## Objective

Identify and launch high-demand, high-profit products for the Pakistani Shopify market using AI-assisted research.

---

## Product Selection Criteria

- Solves a real problem
- High perceived value
- Easy to ship
- Low return rate
- Good profit margin
- Suitable for Cash on Delivery

---

## Research Sources

- TikTok Trends
- Facebook Ads Library
- Daraz
- Shopify Stores
- Google Trends

---

## Success Metrics

- Winning product identified
- Conversion rate
- Profit margin
- Customer satisfaction
- Repeat purchases
