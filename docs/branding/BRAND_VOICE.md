# Trix Commerce Brand Voice

## Version

v1.0

---

# Purpose

Define how Trix Commerce communicates with customers across all platforms.

---

# Brand Personality

Our communication should always be:

- Friendly
- Professional
- Honest
- Helpful
- Confident
- Respectful

---

# Writing Style

- Use simple English.
- Use short and clear sentences.
- Focus on customer benefits.
- Avoid technical jargon.
- Write naturally and professionally.

---

# Customer Communication

Always:

- Answer politely.
- Be transparent.
- Respond quickly.
- Offer practical solutions.
- Build customer trust.

---

# Product Descriptions

Every product description should:

- Explain the problem it solves.
- Highlight key benefits.
- Mention important features.
- Use clear call-to-action.
- Avoid exaggerated promises.

---

# Social Media Voice

Facebook:

- Educational
- Friendly
- Trustworthy

Instagram:

- Visual
- Inspiring
- Modern

TikTok:

- Short
- Engaging
- Easy to understand

WhatsApp:

- Professional
- Quick
- Helpful

---

# Words to Emphasize

- Quality
- Trusted
- Reliable
- Affordable
- Convenient
- Fast Delivery
- Customer Satisfaction

---

# Words to Avoid

- Guaranteed miracle
- Best in the world
- 100% perfect
- Unlimited
- Fake discounts
- Misleading claims

---

# Brand Goal

Build long-term customer trust through honest and consistent communication.
