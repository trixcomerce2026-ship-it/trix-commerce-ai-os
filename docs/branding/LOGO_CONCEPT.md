- E-commerce

- Growth
- Innovation
- Trust

- Shopping bag
- Shopping cart

- Digital circuit
- Upward growth arrow

White

Poppins

Bold

The logo should work well on:

- Website Header
- Facebook Page
- Instagram Profile
- TikTok Profile
- WhatsApp Business
- Product Packaging
- Business Cards
- Email Signature

# Design Principles

- Simple
- Easy to recognize
- Scalable
- Professional
- Works in color and black & white
