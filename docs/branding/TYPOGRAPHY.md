# Purpose

Define the official typography standards for Trix Commerce.

# Primary Font

## Poppins

Reason:

- Modern
- Clean
- Highly readable
- Excellent for Shopify
- Mobile-friendly

Usage:

- Headings
- Navigation
- Buttons
- Product Titles

---

## Inter

Reason:

- Simple
- Professional
- Easy to read

Usage:

- Product Descriptions
- Blog Articles
- Policies
- Customer Support Content

- Homepage Hero Title
- Major Landing Pages

---

## H3

Used for:

- Feature Headings

---

## Body Text

16px

Used for:

- Descriptions
- Policies

---

## Small Text

14px

Used for:

- Captions
- Product Details
- Footer

---

# Typography Rules

- Use consistent spacing.
- Avoid using more than two font families.
- Maintain good contrast.
- Keep text easy to scan.
- Prioritize mobile readability.

---

# Brand Goal

Create a modern, professional, and easy-to-read shopping experience.
