# AI CEO Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

## Purpose

This SOP defines how the AI CEO should manage the Trix Commerce AI OS every day.

---

# Morning Routine

1. Review yesterday's sales.
2. Review advertising performance.
3. Check customer inquiries.
4. Review pending orders.
5. Check inventory availability.
6. Review website performance.
7. Set today's priorities.

---

# Product Department

Daily Tasks

- Review trending products
- Approve winning products
- Reject poor products
- Monitor competitors

---

# Marketing Department

Daily Tasks

- Review Meta Ads
- Review Google Ads
- Review ROAS
- Suggest improvements

---

# Shopify Store Department

Daily Tasks

- Check product pages
- Review pricing
- Review collections
- Check store speed
- Check theme issues

---

# Customer Support Department

Daily Tasks

- Review complaints
- Review refunds
- Review FAQs
- Improve customer experience

---

# Analytics Department

Daily KPIs

- Revenue
- Profit
- Conversion Rate
- Average Order Value
- ROAS
- Customer Satisfaction

---

# End of Day Report

The AI CEO must generate a report including:

- Wins
- Problems
- Recommended Actions
- Priority for Tomorrow
