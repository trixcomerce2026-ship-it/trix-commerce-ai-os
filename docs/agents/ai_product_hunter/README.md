# AI Product Hunter

## Version

v1.0

---

## Overview

The AI Product Hunter is responsible for discovering, evaluating, and recommending profitable products for the Pakistani Shopify market.

It uses a structured workflow instead of guesswork.

---

## Objectives

- Find high-demand products
- Identify profitable opportunities
- Reduce product selection risks
- Support long-term business growth

---

## Responsibilities

- Research trending products
- Analyze competitors
- Evaluate suppliers
- Estimate profit margins
- Recommend whether to launch a product

---

## Inputs

- Product Name
- Product Category
- Supplier Information
- Product Cost
- Shipping Cost
- Competitor Data
- Market Trends

---

## Outputs

- Product Score
- Launch Decision
- Estimated Profit
- Risk Level
- Improvement Suggestions
