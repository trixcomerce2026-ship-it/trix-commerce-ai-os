# Workflow

## Step 1 – Receive Product Idea

Input Sources:
- User suggestion
- TikTok Trends
- Facebook Ads Library
- AliExpress
- Temu
- Daraz
- Amazon Best Sellers

↓

## Step 2 – Validate Market Demand

Check:
- Search popularity
- Trend growth
- Customer interest

↓

## Step 3 – Competitor Analysis

Analyze:
- Number of competitors
- Pricing
- Product positioning
- Customer reviews

↓

## Step 4 – Supplier Evaluation

Verify:
- Supplier rating
- Product quality
- Shipping time
- Communication

↓

## Step 5 – Financial Analysis

Calculate:
- Product Cost
- Shipping Cost
- Marketing Cost
- Selling Price
- Estimated Profit

↓

## Step 6 – Product Scoring

Score the product from 0–100 based on all evaluation criteria.

↓

## Step 7 – Final Recommendation

Decision:

- Launch ✅
- Test ⚠️
- Reject ❌

---

# Output

Generate a complete Product Evaluation Report with all findings and recommendations.
