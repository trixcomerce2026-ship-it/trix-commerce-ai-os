# Calculations

Total Cost =

Product Cost
+ Shipping Cost
+ Packaging Cost
+ Marketing Cost
+ COD Charges
+ Other Expenses

---

Net Profit =

Selling Price - Total Cost

---

Profit Margin (%) =

(Net Profit ÷ Selling Price) × 100
