---

# Market Demand

- [ ] Product has strong demand

- [ ] Not purely seasonal

---

# Competition

- [ ] Competitor research completed
- [ ] Pricing analyzed
- [ ] Market opportunity identified

---

# Supplier Verification

- [ ] Reliable supplier found

- [ ] Acceptable shipping time
- [ ] Clear return policy

# Financial Analysis

- [ ] Product cost calculated

- [ ] Selling price determined
- [ ] Profit margin acceptable

- [ ] Product quality verified
- [ ] Easy to understand
- [ ] Suitable for Cash on Delivery
- [ ] Low return risk
