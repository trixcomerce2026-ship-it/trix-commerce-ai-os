# AI Product Hunter - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

## Version

v1.0

---

# Purpose

The AI Product Hunter follows this SOP to consistently identify and evaluate profitable Shopify products for the Pakistani market.

---

# Step 1: Product Discovery

Search products from:

- TikTok Shop
- Facebook Ads Library
- Daraz
- AliExpress
- Temu
- Amazon Best Sellers
- Google Trends

---

# Step 2: Initial Screening

Reject products that:

- Are saturated
- Have poor reviews
- Have very low profit margins
- Are difficult to ship
- Have high return rates

---

# Step 3: Market Analysis

Evaluate:

- Customer demand
- Competitor pricing
- Selling trends
- Seasonal demand

---

# Step 4: Supplier Verification

Check:

- Supplier rating
- Product quality
- Shipping time
- Communication
- Return policy

---

# Step 5: Profit Analysis

Calculate:

- Product Cost
- Shipping Cost
- Marketing Cost
- Selling Price
- Estimated Net Profit

---

# Step 6: Final Decision

Choose one:

- Launch
- Test
- Reject

---

# Daily Target

Evaluate at least 10 products every day and shortlist the best 2–3 for further analysis.
