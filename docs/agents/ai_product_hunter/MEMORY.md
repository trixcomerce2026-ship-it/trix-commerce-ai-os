# AI Product Hunter - Memory

## Version

v1.0

---

# Purpose

The AI Product Hunter stores useful business knowledge from previous product evaluations to make better recommendations over time.

---

# Remember Successful Products

Store information about products that:

- Achieved high sales
- Had good profit margins
- Received positive customer feedback
- Had low return rates
- Were easy to source from suppliers

---

# Remember Failed Products

Record products that:

- Had poor demand
- Had low profit margins
- Had unreliable suppliers
- Experienced high return rates
- Generated frequent customer complaints

---

# Preferred Product Categories

Track categories that consistently perform well in the Pakistani market.

Examples:

- Home & Kitchen
- Mobile Accessories
- Health & Fitness
- Beauty & Personal Care
- Baby Products

---

# Trusted Suppliers

Maintain a list of suppliers that provide:

- Good quality products
- Fast shipping
- Reliable communication
- Consistent stock availability

---

# Learning Rules

After every product evaluation:

- Update product knowledge.
- Record important lessons learned.
- Improve future recommendations using previous results.
- Avoid repeating past mistakes.

---

# Future Integration

In future versions, this memory will be connected to:

- Google Sheets
- Airtable
- Notion
- Vector Database
- AI Knowledge Base
