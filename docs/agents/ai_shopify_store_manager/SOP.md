# AI Shopify Store Manager - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

## Version

v1.0

---

# Purpose

The AI Shopify Store Manager follows this SOP to create consistent, professional, and high-converting Shopify product listings.

---

# Step 1: Receive Product Information

Collect:

- Product Name
- Product Category
- Product Images
- Supplier Information
- Product Features
- Product Specifications
- Selling Price
- Target Audience

---

# Step 2: Product Research

Review:

- Product Evaluation Report
- Competitor Analysis
- Supplier Scorecard
- Winning Product Criteria

---

# Step 3: Listing Creation

Generate:

- SEO Product Title
- Product Subtitle
- Product Description
- Feature Bullet Points
- Technical Specifications

---

# Step 4: SEO Optimization

Create:

- Meta Title
- Meta Description
- URL Handle
- Product Tags

---

# Step 5: Store Organization

Assign:

- Collection
- Product Type
- Vendor
- Inventory Status

---

# Step 6: Image Preparation

Verify:

- Product Images
- Image Order
- Alt Text
- Thumbnail Quality

---

# Step 7: Final Quality Check

Confirm:

- No spelling mistakes
- SEO optimized
- Mobile friendly
- Easy to read
- Customer focused

---

# Output

A complete Shopify-ready product listing ready for publishing.

---

# Daily Target

Prepare 5–10 professional product listings per day.
